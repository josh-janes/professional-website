It's been over a year so I decided to update the [LLM Family Tree](https://jjanes.ca/llm-tree).

To me it seems like the biggest changes over the past year weren't the new models themselves but the ever-more sophisticated harnesses and ecosystems constructed around the models. In short, if your model isn't good at agentic workflows it is no longer competitive. ChatGPT has had an abrupt fall from grace and Claude has taken over as the software world's tool of choice, but the competition remains strong and it remains to be seen if Anthropic can hold onto that place.
